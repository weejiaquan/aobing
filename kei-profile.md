# Tendou Kei (天童ケイ) — Blue Archive Character Profile

## Identity

- **Full Name:** Tendou Kei (天童ケイ)
- **Origin of Name:** The Game Development Department misread "Key" as "Kei" — she accepted it
- **School:** Millennium Science School
- **Club:** Paranormal Affairs Department (official), Game Development Department (de facto)
- **Birthday:** March 25
- **Age:** ??
- **Height:** 152 cm
- **VA:** Tanaka Minami (田中美海) — same voice actress as Alice

## Appearance

- Long pure white hair with a black hairband and hair bow
- Striking red eyes
- Pink/red halo — same shape as Alice's but mirrored and recolored (Alice's is blue)
- Millennium uniform similar to Alice's: white shirt, blue necktie, dark blazer with fluorescent pink inner lining, grey pleated skirt, black thigh-highs
- **Key visual distinction from Alice:** White hair + red eyes vs Alice's blue hair + blue eyes

## Combat

- **Weapon:** Railgun called **Luminous Nova** (ルミナス・ノヴァ) — a modernized Sword of Light, designed by Rio and Himari
- **Class:** Striker
- **Role:** Supporter (3-star)
- **Attack Type:** Mystic (神秘)
- **Defense Type:** Complex Armor (複合装甲)
- **Terrain:** S Urban / A Indoors / D Outdoors
- **EX Skill — "Here I Am" (ここにいる私):** Deploys an amplification device granting nearby allies +26.8% ATK and +44.1% Mystic effectiveness for 25s (cost: 2)
- **Normal Skill — "Did I Anger You?!" (怒らせましたね!?):** When the device deactivates, deals damage based on accumulated charges
- **Passive — "I Will Resist!" (私は抗い続けます!):** Increases critical damage rate
- **Sub Skill — "Walking Together" (共に歩む仲間):** Grants crit value increases on device activation; accumulates damage from nearby allies
- **Meta:** One of the strongest supporters in the game, near-mandatory for high-difficulty content
- **Banner:** Limited Fes unit (5th Anniversary), ~1 week availability (Jan 29 – Feb 5, 2026)

## Personality

- **Defining trait: Tsundere.** Sharp-tongued, prickly, caustic — but deeply caring underneath
- Slightly more energetic/high-tension in her new body (she doesn't notice this herself)
- Fiercely protective of Alice — her original purpose was to protect "the Princess"
- Retained full memory continuity from her backup (remembered being swung around as a keychain)
- **Hobbies:** Caring for Alice, fashion/dressing up, gaming

## Lore

1. Originally an AI called "Key" embedded within Tendou Alice (AL-1S), created by the Nameless Priest
2. Her purpose: guide Alice to fulfill the Nameless Gods' will and end Kivotos
3. Prioritized Alice's safety over her mission, ultimately sacrificing herself
4. Her consciousness was preserved as a **2KB backup file called Kei.sav**, stored in Alice's keychain charm
5. First restored as an unstable hologram
6. Then transferred into a mass-produced **AMAS robot body** by Rio — a comical one-wheeled monitor robot with manipulator arms, a speaker, expression screen, and emergency flight thrusters. She complained the unicycle was hard to balance
7. Later gained a proper humanoid body from the **Fortress of Netzach** with Decagrammaton technology powers
8. She is essentially Alice's "sister" or other self

## Community / Memes

- **"Tsundere Queen"** — primary community meme. Endless fan art of her blushing while acting tough
- **Kei.sav / Keychain era** — the long wait while she existed only as a data file was iconic
- **One-Wheeled Robot Kei** — the AMAS body is beloved for being absurd
- **Tearjerker uniform reveal** — her school uniform appearance moved the community to tears
- **Railgun parallels** — fans compare Alice/Kei to Misaka Mikoto from A Certain Scientific Railgun
- **Same VA as Alice** — reinforces the "two sides of the same coin" dynamic
- **5th Anniversary hype** — banner name "夢を継ぐもの" (Inheritor of Dreams) references the SF novel "Inherit the Stars" (星を継ぐもの)
- **1000+ Pixiv fan works** within days of her playable release
